## Quality Assurance and Verification

Even in niche markets, quality remains non-negotiable. Each component must meet functional, electrical, and environmental performance standards. Niche distributors and suppliers apply multi-stage testing and documentation to ensure quality.

### Quality Assurance Includes:
1. **Visual and microscopic inspection** for labeling and surface integrity.  
2. **Electrical testing** for compliance with datasheet parameters.  
3. **X-ray and decapsulation analysis** for internal structure validation.  
4. **Functional testing** for design-level performance verification.  
5. **ESD-safe handling and moisture protection** for sensitive components.  

Every part is verified and documented before shipment, ensuring complete confidence in each delivery.
